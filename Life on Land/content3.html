Description of trees

Trees can be also called as lungs of the world as they inhale co2 and exhale o2. O2 is the most important gas for animals and for human beings for respiration in order to gain energy. And also trees help to prevent air pollution.

Trees are the first step of food chains which provide food for all living beings. 80% of human diet is consist with plants. Therefore trees are very important for biodiversity. Every living being depend on trees for their survivsl.

Trees has an important role in water cycle which helps the process of evaporation and facilitate rainfall. So trees are very important to protect fresh water ecosystems and to provide water for living beings for existance.

Trees help to enrich the soil with minerals and stop soil errosion by binding the soil by roots. And also it act as a shield against wind. It helps to prevent climatic disasters like floods and draughts. And also trees provide habitat for animals.

Urbanization, deforestation and expansion of agricultural lands which limit resources and space available for species populations, cause a threat for animal species with extinction. This happens when they miss their habitats, food and water.
